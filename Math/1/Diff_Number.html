Different types of numbers

Natural number

Any whole number from 1 to infinity, sometimes called 'counting numbers'. 0 is not inclued.

e.g. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9

odd number

A whole number that cannot be divided exactly by 2.

e.g. 1, 3, 5, 7, 9

Even number

A whole number that can be fivided exactly by 2.

e.g. 2, 4, 6, 8, 10

Integer

Any of the negative and positive whole numbers, inclueding zero.

-3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3

Prime number

A whole number greater than 1 which only two factors: the number itself and 1.

2, 3, 5, 6, 11

Square number

The product obtained when an integer is multiplied by itself.

1, 4, 9, 16

Fraction

A number representing parts of a whole number, can be written as a common (volgar) fraction in the form of a/b or as a decimal point.

1/2, 1/4, 1/3, 1/8, 13/3, 2 1/2

0.5, 0.2, 0.08, 1.7
