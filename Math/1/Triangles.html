Triangles

A triangle is a plane shape with three three sides and three angles.

Triangles are classified according to the lengths of their sides and the sizes of their angles (or both)

Scalene triangle Scalene triangles have no sides of equal length and no angle that are of equal sizes.
Isosceles triangle Isosceles triangles have two sides of equal length. The angles at the bases of the equal sides are equal in size.
Equilateral triangle Equilateral triangles have three equal sides and three equal angles (each being 60°).

Calculating angles in a triangle

All the angles in a triangle add up to 180°

When finding the exterior angle you would draw a line and the interior angle - 180 = exterior angle
