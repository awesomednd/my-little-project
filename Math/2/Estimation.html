Estimation

It is important to know whether or not an answer that you have obtained it close to the answer you expected. This alows to to epproximate the answer to calculate easily.

To estimate, the numbers you are using need to be rounded before you do the calculation.
Although you can use any accuracy, usuallt the numbers in the calculation are rounded to one significant figure. E.G.

Lets say you have 3.9 × 2.1 ≈ 4 × 2 = 8

Notice that 3.9 × 2.1 = 8.19, so the estimated value of 8 is not too far from the real value
