Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup finely ground graham crackers
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped nuts (such as walnuts or pecans)
  • 1/4 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/4 cup dark rum
  • Extra cocoa powder or powdered sugar for rolling (optional)
Instructions:
  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the ground graham crackers, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and finely chopped nuts.
  2. Add the corn syrup and rum to the dry mixture, and stir until well combined. The mixture should be somewhat crumbly, but should hold together when you press it in your hands.
  3. Roll the mixture into 1-inch balls. If desired, roll the balls in extra cocoa powder or powdered sugar.
  4. Place the rum balls on a parchment-lined baking sheet, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow them to set.
  5. Store the rum balls in an airtight container in the refrigerator until ready to serve. Enjoy!
